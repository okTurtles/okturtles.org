by fiscal year (June 1 - May 31)

Foundation Activities

The okTurtles Foundation funds a number of activities in support of beneficial decentralization technologies.

FY2023

The Foundation's fundraising activities for FY2023 are ongoing.

FY2022

The okTurtles Foundation received $41,757.91, 2.73233405 BTC, 322.15946856 XMR, and 240.8511658 ZEC in new donations during FY2022. During FY2022 the okTurtles Foundation paid out $1,377.20, 4.04971016 BTC and 220.817579881 XMR to support its activities.

  • The foundation requested grant funding from other sources also focused on beneficial open source technology
